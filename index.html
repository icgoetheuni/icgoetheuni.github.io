Intercultural Club-Goethe Uni 

Intercultural Club-Goeth Uni is a student ommunity which promotes dialogue between different cultures, religions and nationalities in Frankfurt and over all Germany. Intercultural Club-Goeth Uni is established by socially responsible group of young people who are studying at Goethe University.
